True Story

Rani Khuman nee Siyali arrived in Austrailia in 1963 from India to continue her librarian studies at the Univercity of New South Whales. She joined her brothers who had come in the 1950's. Siyali had to abandon her mother to continue her education. What she was met by a welcoming nation who accepted her warmly.
