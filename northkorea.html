North Korea

"I think they were trying to escape," Yeonmi says matter-of-factly. "But they didn’t succeed."

North Korea is a communist country ruled by the dictator Kim Jong-Un. As a communist country, North Korea is one of the most isolated societies in the world. No citizen leaves and Hardly anyone but doctors and educators are allowed to travel into the country. Every day in North Korea, its inhabitants endure many hardships like starvation and persecution but The one million troops that stand guard on the border between North and South Korea make it nearly impossible for anyone to leave North Korea. However, between 1993 and 2012, about 24,000 North Korean fugitives were able to escape to South Korea or neighboring countries like Myanmar, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam and Thailand. When Escaping North Korea, escapees choose to leave Korea through a few different routes. But, due to military personnel and the millions of land mines guarding the boarder, escape directly across is not feasible. So, the only way to escape is to undergo the harsh conditions and travel through the mountains on the northwest corner of Korea. The chance of survival of getting across the North Korea/China border is very slim. If defectors dont get caught or shot by soldiers, they still have to survive whatever nature throws at them and crossing a river. If lucky enough to survive the escape across the North Korean border into China, the escapees head to either Mongolia or Beijing. Even though they escaped their harsh life in North Korea, Koreans still have many other problems to worry about. They are rarely accepted into any other society. The Chinese dont want them around, neither do South Koreans. The defectors are always at risk of being captured and forced back into North Korea where they will most likely be put to death. They face discrimination which prevents them from finding a job or a home. As a result of equal hardships outside of their own country, many North Koreans wished for the chance to return back to their country if only they wouldn't be killed.
