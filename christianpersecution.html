Christians Fleeing ISIS

The conflict in the Middle East has brought unrest to many of the people living there. These people -- many from Syria -- have been fleeing to other countries like turkey. Many of these refugees are from the Christian faith. These Christian people are being murder by groups in the Middle East twice as much since 2012 to 2014. The top countries in the Middle East that these Christians face violence in are Syria, Iraq, Afghanistan, Saudi Arabia, Pakistan, Iran, and Yemen. These Christians have almost no where to go in the Middle East. The violence has only been rising, and a large part of that is due to the terrorist group ISIS. ISIS stands for the Islamic State of Iraq and al-Sham. This group will attack cites and take control of them to benefit their cause. While in this city they sometimes relocate the people native to this city. Also many people are just fleeing from the city because of ISIS. Many of these refugees welcome the bombing from the United States to rid ISIS from their city.
